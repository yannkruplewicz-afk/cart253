A full control






Today my friend, let's call him R., my friend R. came to me saying he had spent an hour scrolling and didn't even realize it, after that he was disappointed of this time that has been 'lost' and that he could never get back again. We can't loose our time like that. However, we can't blame social media and algorithms neither because we are the one creating them, they are created for us, to entertain us.
That's when this new solution came into action.

Certified regulation parameters.

Using certified ( controlled, trustable ) regulation systems like parental controls in our social medias would make us be aware of what amount of time we dedicate to it. We could regulate it, control the algorithm's strenght, and maybe orient it in the direction of something we want to learn. This would ultimately help us use social media productively. The control would be on us, the fault would be on us.
I am not talking about parental controls as we know today, but about sophisticated regulation parameters that would be directly in the social media itself.
