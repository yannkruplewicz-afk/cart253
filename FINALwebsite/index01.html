How does it affect us ?


Now you may wonder : Why the hell would i give a fuck about that ? Here you are going to know why you are actually concerned by Surveillance Capitalism, as we all, modern humans, are.

So basically, surveillance capitalism, and the people behind it are like chasing and surveilling you whenever you use your screens. So that's cool if your hermit friend Bob has a new computer, but now he also has new problems.

Before to dive into the three studies above, that contain informations you MIGHT find surprising, let's explain what these problems are.


Surveillance Capitalism is used as a tool to make profit and make business oriented predictions by using your informations. They use and remember EVERYTHING you expose on internet. Who your friends on Facebook are, the new shaver you bought yesterday, and even the last picture you saw on Instagram, remember this, it will be important in the next few minutes.

Then you might say you don't care that people collect your data right ? But what if your data were actually used against yourself ? Check out the studies 1, 2 and 3 to discover more about the topic.
