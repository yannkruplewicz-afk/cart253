What are the already existing solutions ?






Even in this darkness, there is a small candle that still lights up.

'Solutions' have been found, such as the incognito mode on Chrome, which pretends not to use nor remember your data.

Similarly, labels for websites and applications have been created to evaluate how respectful a website or app is regarding privacy, data ethics, and transparency. Among these are the following three:

AFNOR — “Privacy Tech” Label

AFNOR Privacy Tech operates in France and Europe by conducting independent audits to verify that a website or app follows strong GDPR-aligned privacy-by-design practices.


PrivacyTrust Certification

PrivacyTrust Certification is used internationally on websites to show that their published privacy policy accurately reflects how they actually collect, store, and use personal data.


Digital Trust Label

Digital Trust Label is used primarily in Switzerland and Europe for digital services that want to prove transparent, safe, and fair user treatment across security, data protection, and interface design.
