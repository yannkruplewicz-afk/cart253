What are OUR solutions ?



2 solutions are possible for us if we want to get rid of this problem which is not ours
(Because we can only be the victims there, remember), if we want to solve it, that's another story .






Remember before internet ?

Honestly, people seem to be happier before internet, that's what my grandparents say, no need for study this time. A solution could simply be to leave for St Pierre et Miquelon, in the nature, no internet, no problem. That might seem frightening because we are so used to our comfortable lives, I don't even know if some human communities still live in the nature, but at least, they would not have to care about the problem of surveillance Capitalism. They would just breathe some fresh and pure air, laying on a palm leaves hammock.





If..You want to keep your phone

If the first solution didn't appeal to you, you might then have to rely on your device only on purpose, which means only when you actually need it, so that you can limit distractions as well as stopping your dependence to screens. The idea is basically the same as in the previous idea, stopping the screens' dependance, being more 'natural', thinking twice before using it. That means, getting rid of the frivolous things like social media. Also you might need to be mentally stronger. In the digital Age, you can be influenced everywhere, by everyone. That's the point of Surveillance Capitalism.
