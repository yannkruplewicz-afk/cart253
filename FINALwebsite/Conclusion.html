Here we are, 2026


“If you’ve got nothing to hide, you are nothing.”
Shoshana Zuboff, The age of Surveillance Capitalism

What does that mean ? According to Zuboff it means that a person who has “nothing to hide” is a person stripped of privacy, agency, and individuality. In a surveillance system, that person becomes “nothing” because their humanity has been reduced to data.

There is a lot to be done about this issue which is growing faster and faster, no need to predict how big the problem will be in 2030, we all want to sleep tonight. But we all have to be aware of the existence of Surveillance Capitalism because as Zuboff also said :"It is no longer enough to automate information flows about us; the goal now is to automate us". In other words, be aware or be manipulated.
