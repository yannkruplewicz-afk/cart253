New jobs ?






Today, november the 24th of 2025, we are seeing AI growing. AI will make Surveillance Capitalism even more efficient, is going to replace and make disappear lots of jobs, a solution could be to create new jobs.

Surveilling the Surveillance

These new jobs would aim at surveilling the apps / websites / companies to prevent surveillance Capitalism. Internet spyers in some way.

Some would show up in companies and check if everything is alright, if there is no 'excess', defined at the precise moment when an individual's personnal liberty is repressed, like when stealing data or manipulating through algorithms.

That solution would create a balance between the capitalists and the people, otherwise, the balance will be inexisting, even worse than what we know today.
